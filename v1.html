Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Walk With Jesus?

A community event walking along the beach to raise funds and awareness for Salty Farm Ministries’ outreach.

Can I participate without walking?

Absolutely! You can support the mission by making a donation directly through our website.

Who can participate?

Everyone is welcome! Families, church groups, and corporate teams are encouraged to join. No pets allowed on this part of the beach.
