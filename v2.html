The Mission

A shared walk of faith that turns compassion into visible action.

Event Details

Walk the beach. Share the hope. Make a difference.

  • The walk begins and ends behind Sharky’s restaurant.
  • Participants can choose a 1-mile or 2-mile beach walk route.
  • Families, churches, businesses, and teams are welcome.
  • No pets are allowed on this part of the beach.
  • Bring comfortable shoes, water, sunscreen, and a positive spirit.

FAQ

What walkers, donors, and sponsors need to know.

It is a community event where participants walk together along the beach to raise funds and awareness for Salty Farm Ministries’ outreach to individuals experiencing homelessness.
The walk begins and ends behind Sharky’s restaurant on October 10. The walk kicks off at 10:00 AM, with awards and recognition around 1:00 PM.
Everyone is welcome, including individuals, families, church groups, businesses, and teams. The event is family-friendly; however, please note that no pets are allowed on this part of the beach.
Registration may be free, but participants are encouraged to fundraise to support the mission.
Fundraising is encouraged but not required. Every participant is invited to set a personal goal and help the community reach the overall fundraising goal.
All proceeds support Salty Farm Ministries’ outreach efforts, including food, showers, clothing, discipleship, support services, and care for individuals experiencing homelessness.
Yes. Supporters can donate directly through the website even if they cannot attend or walk in person.
Yes. Businesses and organizations can partner as sponsors through the various levels listed on this site.
You can email Salty Farm Ministries at info@saltyfarmministries.org for more information.
